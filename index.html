Melyra Database Editor

Manage & Edit assets from the Melyra Project!

  • View: see the list of assets, or open a specific one!
  • Actions: do actions on the database, mainly pushing it to update.

This tool's use is to make the existence of Melyra's objects (items, creatures, recipe) less abstract for developers.
With this, we will be able to manage each object's data directly here. Pushing to update will export the data in the datapack.
Keep in mind specific behavior will have to be manually added (e.g abilities, creatures with special AI, etc.)
